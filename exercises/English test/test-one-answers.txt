## PART 3: GRAMMAR (20 points)

**16.** If we _________________ (work) from home more often, we would save a lot of time on commuting.
   - **Answer: B (worked)**
   - *Explanation: Second conditional requires past tense in the if-clause*

**17.** The employees who _________________ (choose) to work remotely must have reliable internet connections.
   - **Answer: C (have chosen)**
   - *Explanation: Present perfect to indicate an action completed and relevant to the present*

**18.** "I wish I _________________ (could / can) attend the meeting in person," said Maria.
   - **Answer: A (could)**
   - *Explanation: After "wish," we use past tense to express present wishes*

**19.** Despite the advantages of remote work, ___________ is still a need for face-to-face interaction.
   - **Answer: A (there)**
   - *Explanation: "There is" is the existential construction needed here*

**20.** The survey suggests that _________________ (most / most of the) employees prefer flexible working arrangements.
   - **Answer: A (most)**
   - *Explanation: "Most" is used without "of the" when referring to a general group*

## PART 4: TRUE/FALSE (10 points)

**21.** According to the text, productivity always decreases when employees work from home.
   - **Answer: F (False)**
   - **Reason:** Some studies indicate productivity has actually improved with remote work. The text states: "Productivity has not decreased as many feared; in fact, some studies indicate it has improved."

**22.** Hybrid working means employees never come to the office.
   - **Answer: F (False)**
   - **Reason:** Hybrid working means employees come to the office on specific days but also work from home. The text states: "Employees come to the office on specific days for collaboration and team-building, but have flexibility to work from home for focused individual tasks."

**23.** Companies like Shopify are using the hybrid working model.
   - **Answer: T (True)**
   - **Reason:** The text explicitly mentions these companies have implemented the hybrid model with positive results: "Tech companies like Shopify and Dropbox have already implemented this model with positive results."

**24.** All industries will eventually adopt remote working.
   - **Answer: F (False)**
   - **Reason:** The text states that fields requiring hands-on work will maintain more traditional office environments. The text says: "Fields requiring hands-on work or immediate collaboration will maintain more traditional office environments, while knowledge-based industries will embrace more flexible arrangements."

**25.** The text suggests that maintaining company culture is easier with remote work.
   - **Answer: F (False)**
   - **Reason:** The text indicates it's harder to maintain company culture and team cohesion with remote work. The text states: "Managers struggle with maintaining team cohesion and company culture when employees are scattered across different locations."
